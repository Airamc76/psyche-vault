- Bandura, A., Ross, D. & Ross, S.A. (1961). "Transmission of aggression through imitation." *Journal of Abnormal and Social Psychology*. El experimento del muñeco Bobo.
- Cialdini et al. papers sobre cumplimiento e influencia.
